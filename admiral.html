Admiral

"All we have to decide is what to do with the time that is given us."-Gandalf

Admiral has been playing Dungeons & Dragons since 2007 starting with 3.5e.

His first character was a half-elf ranger most famous for dying in spectacularly bizarre and inconvenient ways. Through Dungeons & Dragons and fantasy universes like Middle-Earth, Alagaësia and so on, Admiral developed a fascination with other cultures and customs. This led to a major in Anthropology and Economics along with a love of traveling.

Admiral began to take on the role of Dungeon Master in 2017 starting with a home-brew campaign in the 5e setting. Since then, they have hosted numerous campaigns, including running Curse of Strahd for the G.O.D.S. team.

What once began as a chance encounter to play as a Clash team in League of Legends has now lead to a flurry of D&D activity with multiple people catching the bug and even DMing themselves. Now, Admiral can finally enjoy playing characters again, notably "Roscoe Strongbones" a halfling fighter and "Dmitri Von Riegl" a human fighter both in different campaigns run by Daedru. If not playing D&D, the G.O.D.S. team is probably practicing for Clash once again.

D&D, League, Economic Trend Forcasting...

We have a very close knit community of avid D&D players, we play using the roll20 system and audio through our discord server.

If you enjoy D&D and want to have a good time and play, check it out and apply to join! We're always looking for good players & DM's!
